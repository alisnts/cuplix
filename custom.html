Cupcake Customization

At Cup`lix, we believe every celebration deserves cupcakes as unique as the moment itself. Whether you're planning a birthday, wedding, corporate event, or any special occasion, we offer full cupcake customization to bring your vision to life.

Customization Options

Flavors: Choose from our wide variety of delicious flavors — from classic vanilla and decadent chocolate fudge to matcha green tea and salted caramel.

Frostings: Select your favorite frosting type and color. Whether you prefer creamy buttercream, rich cream cheese, or light whipped frosting, we’ve got you covered.

Fillings: Add an extra surprise inside your cupcakes! Popular options include chocolate ganache, strawberry jam, and caramel filling.

Decorations: Personalize your cupcakes with sprinkles, edible toppers, themed designs, and custom messages. Perfect for birthdays, anniversaries, and more!

Packaging: Choose between our elegant cupcake boxes or custom-designed packaging that matches the theme of your event.

How to Order Custom Cupcakes

Custom cupcake orders must be placed at least 5 days in advance. You can send us your design ideas or inspiration photos, and we’ll work closely with you to create cupcakes that fit your event perfectly.

To start your customization, simply message us or visit our store to discuss your ideas with our team!

Important Notes

  • Minimum order quantity may apply for custom designs.
  • Special requests are subject to availability of materials and ingredients.
  • Full payment is required at the shop upon pickup.
