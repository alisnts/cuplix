Allergen Information

At Cup`lix, your safety and satisfaction are our top priorities. We want to make sure you have all the information you need when enjoying our cupcakes.

Please note:

Our cupcakes are made in a kitchen that uses the following ingredients:

  • Dairy (milk, butter, cream)
  • Eggs
  • Wheat (gluten)
  • Soy
  • Peanuts
  • Tree Nuts (such as almonds, pecans, walnuts, coconut)

Cross-Contamination Warning:

While we take great care in cleaning our equipment and preparing each order, cross-contamination may still occur. Our products are not produced in a certified allergen-free kitchen.

Special Requests:

If you have a food allergy or dietary restriction, please inform us before placing your order. We will do our best to accommodate, but we cannot guarantee a 100% allergen-free product.

Important Reminder:

Always prioritize your health and safety. If you have a severe allergy, we advise consulting with our team directly before ordering.
