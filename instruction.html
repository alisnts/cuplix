Cupcake Care Instructions

To enjoy your Cup`lix cupcakes at their best, a little care goes a long way! Hereâ€™s how to keep them fresh and delicious:

Same-Day Enjoyment

Our cupcakes are made fresh daily and are best enjoyed on the same day of pickup. If you plan to eat them within a few hours, simply keep them at room temperature, away from direct sunlight and heat.

Storing for Later

If you need to store your cupcakes overnight, place them in an airtight container and keep them in a cool, dry place. For cupcakes with cream cheese or fresh fruit toppings, we recommend refrigerating them and letting them come to room temperature before serving for the best flavor and texture.

Freezing Cupcakes

Want to save some for later? You can freeze our cupcakes! Place them in a freezer-safe container for up to 2 weeks. To enjoy, thaw them at room temperature for a few hours before eating.

Handling Tips

Always handle your cupcakes gently and keep them upright to preserve their decorations. If transporting them, make sure they are in a secure box to prevent movement.

Freshness Promise

At Cup`lix, we put love and care into every cupcake we bake. Following these simple care tips will help you enjoy every bite just as we intended!
