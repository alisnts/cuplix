FAQS

Where are you located?

Cup`lix is located in Sto. Rosario St, Capas, Tarlac, Philippines. All orders are for self-pickup only at our main shop.

How do I place an order?

You can place your order through our website by selecting your cupcake flavors and box size on the Order Page. Once submitted, we'll prepare your cupcakes for pickup at your chosen time.

Do you offer delivery?

Currently, we do not offer delivery. All cupcakes are available for pickup only at our store.

What are your payment options?

Payments are made in-store upon pickup. We accept cash and GCash payments at the shop.

When should I place my order?

We recommend placing your order at least one day in advance to ensure availability and freshness. Same-day pickups may be possible depending on stock.

Are your cupcakes baked fresh?

Yes! All our cupcakes are baked fresh daily using premium ingredients with no preservatives.

Do your cupcakes contain allergens?

Some cupcakes may contain dairy, gluten, eggs, peanuts, or tree nuts. Please check each flavor’s description or ask our team in-store for more info.

How should I store my cupcakes after pickup?

Room Temperature: Best enjoyed within 1 day

Refrigerated: Keeps well for up to 3 days in an airtight container

Can I cancel or change my order?

You may cancel or modify your order up to 24 hours before your scheduled pickup time.

Still have questions?

Feel free to visit us in-store or contact us via the form on our Contact Page. We’d love to help you!
